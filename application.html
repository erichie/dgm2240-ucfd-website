    Application Process

    Thank you for your interest in employment with Utah County Fire. UCFD is a professional organization that prides itself on providing quality service to all Utah County citizens and the communities that we serve. We are looking for motivated, hardworking, and capable individuals to join our team.

    Our fire season locally runs from June 1st to October 1st. We will run, on average, 150 to 200 fire calls a year. During the height of the fire season, we employ up to 80 people. Our staff is diversified, in that we hire from local fire departments, colleges and area residents.

    Those interested in applying for UCFD must:

    • Be 18 years of age
    • Possess a valid Utah drivers license
    • Pass arduous physical test, drug screen, and background check
    • Complete wildland certification courses
    • Have a high school diploma or equivalency certificate

    Applications are accepted year-round, although, applications received after February 15th may not be considered until the following Calendar year (exceptions are made for those with advanced qualifications). If you are interested in applying for our seasonal/time limited positions we look forward to hearing from you.
